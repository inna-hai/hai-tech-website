How it works

Create a new session in just a few simple steps

A parent, educator, guide, or therapist can define a precise activity with ease — and build an experience for one child or a whole group.

01 · GoalDefine what you want the activity to achieve.
02 · SkillsChoose which abilities you want to strengthen.
03 · TasksCreate the right challenges and scenarios.
04 · SuccessDefine what counts as progress or success.
05 · FitAdapt the experience to each child or group.
06
During play

While children play,
AI agents are active in the background.

Each child receives personal guidance.

The AI agents encourage, direct, provide hints when needed, and adjust challenge level based on progress and defined goals.

What the AI does

  • Encourages and guides
  • Provides hints at the right moment
  • Adjusts pace and difficulty
  • Detects patterns and progress

What do parents and professionals get?

At the end of every session, a personal progress picture is created.

Not only whether the child succeeded
But also how they moved through the process.
How they collaborated
How they joined in, listened, initiated, and responded to others.
How they handled challenges

Where support is needed next
So the next activity can be planned more precisely.

Market opportunity

We sit at the intersection of 3 major markets

Gaming already exists at massive scale

Minecraft is one of the largest game platforms in the world, with estimates of more than 200 million monthly users in 2025. We do not need to teach children a new habit — they are already there.

SEL and EdTech are growing

The social-emotional learning market is worth billions of dollars and growing fast, as education systems, therapists, and parents search for measurable tools to support emotional and social development.

Initial audience
Families of autistic children, therapists, social groups, and special education settings.
Expanding audience
Schools, counselors, SEL programs, enrichment centers, and group facilitators.
Market vision
Any framework that wants to turn digital playtime into an opportunity for social and personal growth.

Business model

The model is built on recurring revenue and multi-channel expansion

Revenue engines

  • Monthly subscription for families
  • Licensing for professionals and group facilitators
  • Institutional licensing for schools and therapy centers
  • Implementation services, content, and premium sessions

Why it works

  • Ongoing value, not a one-time purchase
  • Data, personalization, and measurable progress improve retention
  • The same product engine can serve B2C, B2B, and B2G
  • Natural expansion from content into tasks, reports, and professional guidance
In the first phase, the initial target audience is parents and professionals. Later, expansion moves into schools and broader education systems.
